DIY Drone Thrust Testing

I’m exploring drone designs, with a focus on: 1) long flight times, 2) inexpensive, 3) capable of “good” position hold.  One key aspect of any drone is the choice of motor and props.  

 

Another factor here is the battery.  I’ve built some packs using VTC6 18650 cells, which are rated for 15A continuous or 30A burst current.  So it’s important that my motor/prop combination doesn’t violate these limits.
